About BHODI

"BHODI(Betterment of Our Community Through Hope and Inspiration) is a social outreach initiative organized by the Department of Computer Science at Mahaguru College of Engineering.BHODI main initative is STEP a program designed to provide comprehensive skill development through hands-on training, workshops, and seminars, focusing on enhancing technical abilities in various fields such as programming, digital literacy, and office automation. By reaching out to local communities, students, and professionals, BHODI aims to bridge the digital divide and empower individuals with the tools and knowledge necessary to succeed in a technology-driven world. The program also emphasizes inclusivity, ensuring that participants from diverse backgrounds can benefit from the training, contributing to both personal growth and community development."

Our Latest Programs

Office automation refers to the use of technology and software to streamline and automate routine office tasks like data management, communication, scheduling, and document processing. It boosts productivity, reduces manual errors, and enhances workflow efficiency in the workplace.

Digital literacy is the ability to effectively use digital tools, technologies, and the internet to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information. It involves understanding how to navigate online platforms, manage digital content, and ensure privacy and security.

Web designing in education focuses on creating engaging, accessible, and user-friendly websites that enhance learning experiences. It combines visual design with intuitive navigation to make educational content easy to explore and interact with across all devices.

Basic programming involves writing simple code using concepts like variables, loops, and functions to instruct computers. It helps solve problems and automate tasks, using languages like Python or JavaScript.

Data entry involves the process of inputting, updating, and managing information in digital formats, often using spreadsheets, databases, or specialized software. It requires accuracy and attention to detail, as the data entered is critical for decision-making, record-keeping, and operations in various industries.

Poster designing is the art of creating visually appealing graphics to convey messages or promote events. It combines layout, color, typography, and imagery to capture attention and communicate information effectively. A good poster balances aesthetics and clarity, ensuring the key message stands out to engage the audience.
